    (1989) Dragon Ball Z Abridged is a fan-made parody series by TeamFourStar that humorously condenses and reimagines the original Dragon Ball Z anime. Through witty writing and voice acting, it delivers a hilarious take on iconic characters and moments from the series while maintaining a deep respect for the source material. The parody highlights the epic battles, pop culture references, and inside jokes, making it an entertaining and nostalgic watch for Dragon Ball Z fans and newcomers alike.

    SUZUME

    (2022) Japanese animated fantasy film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai. It follows 17-year-old Suzume Iwato and young stranger Souta Munakata as they join forces to prevent disasters caused by a colossal, supernatural worm. Produced by CoMix Wave Films and distributed by Toho, the film received positive reviews for its animation, characters, emotional depth, and music. Despite criticism for its similarity to Shinkai's previous works, it became the fourth highest-grossing film of 2022 in Japan, grossing over $323.9 million worldwide.
